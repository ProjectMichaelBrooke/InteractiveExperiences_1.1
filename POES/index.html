POES
Personal Open Elective Stats
      POES works differently than any other tabletop game system, in a way that allows you to easily make your own setting.

      It has four stats whose names you enter, a series of non-copyright images to used for NPCS and the ability to fill in the blanks to describe rooms, people and situations. As long as the setting is not too large, POES should be able to transform itself into any kind of setting you want.
      Just keep in mind that POES is meant to be used by people of many different ages. If you play a POES digital RPG and feel that it contains conent that is not appropriate for kids, please click on ‘flag as Adults Only’ and the entry will be.
      The default stats for POES are Strength, Agility, Magic and Wits, and all things in the game fit into one of those four categories. The names can be changed, but all skills learned in the game go into one of those categories, and if your character learns something in a stat they are low in, they will struggle to do it no matter how good their teacher was.
      Default games give good ideas on how to rename the stats and describe entities, as well as containing a basic combat system. Albionus has Combat, Stealth, Knowledge and Erudition,for example.
      Just remember when making your skill lists to ask, “What stat does that fit in?” and you will do fine.
